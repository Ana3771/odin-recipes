Description

Delicious Burger recipe super easy to do!

Ingrendients

  • Ground beef
  • Seasonings
  • Bread for patty
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Make a Sauce for your patties
  2. Shape your ground beef into patties
  3. On a grill cook the patties for over 6 minutes
  4. Build your burger with bread, patty, cheese and close
  5. Enjoy you patty!
